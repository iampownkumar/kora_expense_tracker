Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

Kora Expense Tracker is built with your privacy as the primary focus. We believe your financial data should be yours alone. This policy outlines how our application treats your data and the specific device permissions required to provide our service.

Permissions & Usage

For your transparency, here are the system-level permissions our application uses and the specific reasons why:

android.permission.CAMERA Camera Access

Required to enable the capture of physical receipt images. These images are attached to your transactions for better record keeping. We only access the camera when you explicitly choose to "Take a Photo" within the transaction editor.

android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Read Storage Access

Used to allow you to select existing receipt images or PDF documents from your gallery and phone storage to attach to transactions.

android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Write Storage Access

Required for the Import/Export functionality. This allows the app to save CSV backups and PDF reports to your device's global Downloads folder for easy access.

Data Collection

Zero Server Storage: Kora Expense Tracker is a "Local-First" application. All transaction records, account details, and images are stored solely on your device's internal memory.

No Tracking: We do not use third-party tracking scripts, analytics, or advertising IDs. Your spending habits and financial health are never shared with us or any third party.

Your Choices

You may revoke any of the permissions listed above at any time through your Android System Settings. Note that doing so may limit your ability to use certain features like receipt capture or data backup.
